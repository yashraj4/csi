The team responsible for each literary content, from social media content to magazine articles, only the most eloquent and those with flair to their writing are selected.

Also called the Fun ones of CSI, this team is the brains behind all events conducted by UPES-CSI. It is also responsible for handling and ensuring the smooth execution of each event. This committee is known for its perfectionism and being extremely innovative with event ideas and execution; outdoing themselves with each passing event.
These people see what no one else sees. They are all about that perfect shot. This committee is for the shutterbugs who capture the world through their lens. This team is responsible for capturing all the moments of UPES-CSI as well as judging all the photography events conducted by UPES-CSI.
The technical committee is the one handling the website and creating and managing the technical events. Usually, the ones working behind the curtain, the technical committee is one of the most important ones, especially in a technical chapter.
This committee manages and records all registrations, responses, and queries as well as logs them in the database. This team is the information receiver of UPES-CSI, helping us in making each event better than its predecessor by providing invaluable input.
This team is like the back end of the chapter. Maintaining event reports, minutes of meetings, event hosting, and being the lubricant that makes all teams run smoothly fall under its area of expertise. This team is usually the one willing to go the extra mile.
